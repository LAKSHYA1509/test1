WHY ARPAN

Blood Donation is a critcal process that saves millions of lives each year. However, according to the National Health Profile 2019, published by Central Bureau of Health and Intellignece in India, the annual blood requirement in the country is estimated to be around 12 million units, out of which only 10 million units are collected
Well Blood Banks can only comply 2.5 uints of blood per 1000 eligible people and this number, thanks to COVID, dipped lower in 2019.

Actually, the blood stock in blood banks increases at very slow rate, but with the help of this initiative, a rapid growth in stocks of blood will be observed in blood banks as well.

Now the actual question arises, that why we chose to start this initiative. Well, there are many reasons, and first of them is to fulfill the current demand and overcome the shortage of Blood in India. Along with this, the infrastructre of Blood Donation in India will also develop.

HOW

ARPAN

WORKS?

ARPANTM works as a bridge between a blood donor and a patient in need. There are three ways to Login/Sign Up on this platform, as a patient,hospital or as a donor. ARPANnotifies all the registered blood donors withing a specified radius of 3Kms. from the location where Blood has been demanded. It could be a patient or even a hospital.

The donors are then notified about the demand and then first 20 registering donors are specified for the blood need. After that, donor entries for a particular patient stops.

The donor is then rewarded with GIFT VOUCHERS or even CRYPTOCURRENCY as a bonus and then given a rest for 4 months.
